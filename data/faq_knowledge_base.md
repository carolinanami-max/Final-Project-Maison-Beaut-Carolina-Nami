## Maison Beauté Policies

### Returns
Pre-loved items are accepted for return within 14 days of delivery if the product does not match its described condition. Items must be returned in the same condition as received. Opened cosmetics are non-returnable unless there is a discrepancy with the listing.

### Authenticity
All products sold on Maison Beauté are authenticated by our team before listing. We do not accept counterfeit items.

### Shipping
We ship across the EU. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at checkout. International shipping outside the EU is available for select destinations.

### Allergen Disclaimer
Maison Beauté provides ingredient information as listed by sellers and on product packaging. We are not responsible for individual allergic reactions. Customers with known allergies should consult the full ingredient list and, where in doubt, perform a patch test. If you experience any adverse reaction, please contact us immediately.

### Sustainability Commitment
Every pre-loved purchase reduces beauty waste. Maison Beauté is committed to circular beauty — extending the life of luxury products and keeping them out of landfill.
